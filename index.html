About Us

Building Dreams Into Reality

AG ARCHITECTS AND INTERIOR DESIGNERS is a premier architecture and design firm based in Delhi, dedicated to creating spaces that seamlessly blend form, function, and sustainability. Our multidisciplinary approach encompasses architecture, interior design, and urban planning.

Located in the heart of North Delhi at Rana Pratap Bagh, we serve clients across Delhi NCR with innovative design solutions tailored to contemporary lifestyles and business needs. Our expertise spans residential villas, commercial complexes, retail spaces, and sustainable architecture.

With a team of experienced architects and designers, we transform visions into architectural masterpieces that stand the test of time. Every project reflects our commitment to excellence, attention to detail, and deep understanding of spatial dynamics.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our architectural and interior design services.

What architectural services does AG Architects provide?
We offer comprehensive architectural services including residential architecture (villas, bungalows, apartments), commercial architecture (office buildings, retail spaces), sustainable design, 3D visualization, interior design, landscape architecture, and complete project management from concept to completion.
How long does a typical architecture project take?
Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. Design development typically takes 4-8 weeks, approvals 2-4 weeks, and construction 6-18 months depending on project size. We provide detailed timelines during the initial consultation and maintain transparent communication throughout the project.
What is the cost of architectural services in Delhi?
Our fees are structured based on project scope, complexity, and services required. Architectural design fees typically range from 5-10% of construction cost. Interior design projects vary based on area and finishes. We offer transparent pricing and provide detailed quotations after understanding your requirements during the free consultation.
Do you provide 3D visualization before construction?
Yes! We create detailed 3D photorealistic renderings and virtual walkthroughs for all our projects. This allows you to visualize spaces, materials, lighting, and finishes before construction begins, ensuring complete satisfaction with the design and enabling informed decision-making throughout the process.
Which areas in Delhi NCR do you serve?
We serve all areas across Delhi NCR including North Delhi, South Delhi, East Delhi, West Delhi, Gurgaon, Noida, Greater Noida, Faridabad, and Ghaziabad. Our office is conveniently located in Rana Pratap Bagh, making us easily accessible for consultations and site visits throughout the NCR region.
Do you handle municipal approvals and permissions?
Absolutely! We handle all aspects of municipal approvals including building plan submissions, NOCs, fire safety clearances, and other regulatory requirements. Our team has extensive experience working with various municipal authorities across Delhi NCR, ensuring smooth and timely approval processes.
Can you work within my budget constraints?
Yes, we work with various budget ranges and offer scalable solutions tailored to your financial parameters. During our consultation, we discuss your budget and requirements to create designs that maximize value while maintaining quality. We provide transparent cost breakdowns and help optimize designs to achieve the best results within your budget.
What makes AG Architects different from other firms?
Our unique combination of innovative design thinking, sustainable practices, and personalized service sets us apart. We integrate advanced 3D visualization, maintain transparent communication, offer comprehensive project management, and focus on creating timeless designs that blend functionality with aesthetics. Our 20+ years of experience and commitment to client satisfaction ensure exceptional results.
